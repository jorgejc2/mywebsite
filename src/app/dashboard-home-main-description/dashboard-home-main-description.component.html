Taking AP Computer Science Principles in high school is what peaked my interest in programming and led me to pursue computer engineering. I was more enticed by the software aspect at the time and seeing how much of a creative outlet it gave people. I remember my first dream was creating a gaming app for the app store and having it trend.

My only issue at the time was that I had never coded in my life and felt I wasn’t capable and didn’t have the knowledge to do so.

Despite that, I applied and got accepted to U of I for computer engineering.

The courses I’ve taken have only empowered me and helped me grow in confidence with my ability to code and work with hardware.

I’ve grown a deep appreciation for computers and the impact they can have around us. All I hope for is a career where I can continue to learn and grow, and make a meaningful impact on whatever projects I tackle.
